The Story of Her Becoming a Singer

AURORA is a Norwegian singer and songwriter. She started writing songs when she was just 6 years old. At first, she didn’t want to become famous — she just loved music. When she was 16, one of her songs was uploaded online by her family. It quickly got attention from music producers. Soon, she was discovered and signed to a record label. Her unique voice and emotional songs made her popular around the world. AURORA says she makes music to help people feel something deep inside.
